Boiler Water Treatment

Flagship Product

AQUADET-121 / 122 Series

Our boiler compounds are formulated as multifunctional liquid treatments for low to medium pressure boilers. Unlike generic treatments, AQUADET-121 utilizes organic-inorganic chelating agents that actively seek out mineral ions before they can form scale.

Advanced Sludge Conditioning: Incorporates synthetic polymers that distort the crystal lattice of hardness salts, keeping them as a fluid, non-adherent sludge for easy removal via blow-down.
Metal Passivation: Features a "glossy phosphate" based inhibitor system that forms a protective microscopic film on internal metal surfaces, effectively halting corrosion.

Dual-Action Protection

AQUADET-131 / 133 / 134

Cooling towers are prone to rapid bio-fouling and scale. The AQUADET program provides a 360-degree shield against the factors that cause cooling system failure.

AQUADET-131 Oxidizing Biocide: A stabilized bromine-based formula that remains active even in high pH or ammonia-contaminated systems where traditional chlorine fails.
AQUADET-134 Bio-Dispersant: A specialized non-ionic surfactant that penetrates and loosens tough bio-slimes, allowing biocides to reach the metal surface for 100% sterilization.

Membrane Longevity Specialist

AQUADET-141 / 144 Technology

Membrane replacement is the highest cost in water treatment. AQUADET antiscalants are designed to extend membrane life by 300% by managing silica and sulfate saturation levels.

AQUADET-141 Antiscalant: A polymer-free organic molecule that handles Silica up to 120 PPM and prevents Carbonate/Sulphate scales in the reject water stream.
CIP Recovery (143/144): Our alkaline and acidic cleaners restore flux by dissolving inorganic sulfates (Barium/Strontium) and removing complex organic bio-debris.
