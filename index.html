About AQUADET CORPORATION

AQUADET CORPORATION manufactures and supplies industrial water treatment chemicals designed to control scaling, corrosion and microbiological growth in industrial systems. Our chemicals are used in boilers, cooling towers, reverse osmosis plants and wastewater treatment facilities.

Boiler Chemicals

Anti-scalants, corrosion inhibitors, sludge conditioners and oxygen scavengers for efficient boiler performance.

Cooling Tower Chemicals

Biocides, biodispersants and scale inhibitors to maintain clean cooling water systems.

RO & Membrane Chemicals

Antiscalants and membrane cleaning chemicals to protect RO systems and improve efficiency.
