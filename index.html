130 SERIES: Cooling Tower Management

AQUADET-131

Oxidizing Biocide
  • Chemistry: Stabilized Bromine-based liquid biocide.
  • Efficiency: Highly active in high pH (Alkaline) waters and ammonia-contaminated systems.
  • Profile: Environmentally friendly; white to pale yellow liquid appearance.
Dosage: 30 PPM based on makeup water
Miscibility: Complete in water
Application: Twice weekly slug dose
Stability: Excellent shelf stability

AQUADET-132

Non-Oxidizing Biocide
  • Target: Broad-spectrum microbiological control including algae and fungi.
  • Synergy: Often used in rotation with 131 to prevent microbial resistance.
Dosage: 40 PPM of total system volume
Chemistry: CMIT/MIT Based
pH Range: Effective across broad ranges

AQUADET-133

Scale & Corrosion Inhibitor
  • Protection: Specifically protects Yellow Metals (Copper) and Mild Steel.
  • Mechanism: Organo-phosphate based inhibitor prevents hardness scale formation.
Dosage: 30 PPM in re-circulating water
System: Open Re-circulating Cooling Water
Stability: High Alkalinity stability

AQUADET-134

Bio-Dispersant
  • Function: Penetrates and fluidizes biological slime for removal.
  • Safety: Non-corrosive, non-foaming, and biodegradable alkyl diamine.
Dosage: 16 PPM in re-circulating water
Type: Surfactant/Dispersant
Action: Enhances biocide efficacy

140 SERIES: RO Membrane Solutions

AQUADET-141 / 142

RO Antiscalants
  • 141: Silica Specialist (handles up to 120 PPM in reject water). Polymer-free organic formula.
  • 142: High TDS Specialist (designed for feed water up to 5000 PPM TDS).
Dosage: 2 to 5 PPM in Feed Water
Dose Point: Prior to micron filter
Compatibility: All PA Membranes

AQUADET-143 / 144

CIP Membrane Cleaners
  • 143 (Alkaline): Removes organic debris and bio-fouling.
  • 144 (Acidic): Removes insoluble sulphates (Barium, Strontium, Calcium) and Carbonates.
pH (144): < 2.0 (1% Solution)
CIP Concentration: 3% - 4% solution
