Boiler Treatment Compounds

AQUADET-121

Anti-Scalant / Sludge Conditioner

Ideally formulated compound based on organic-inorganic chelating agents containing sludge conditioner & corrosion inhibitor.

Performance Benefits

  • Anti-Scalent: Polymeric compounds form a coating on precipitate, preventing adhesion to boiler surfaces.
  • Sludge Conditioning: Chelating action forms water-soluble sludge for efficient removal via normal blow-down.
  • Corrosion Inhibition: Contains dispersants and glossy phosphate inhibitors to prevent metal degradation.

Technical Profile

  • Physical Form: Liquid
  • Mechanism: Chelating & Surface Coating
  • Function: Inhibition of scale & surface protection

AQUADET-122

Smoke Tube Boiler Specialist

Designed for internal treatment of water in smoke tube boilers using soft water.

Performance Benefits

  • Deposition Control: Polymeric sludge conditioner fluidizes existing sludge for removal during blow-down.
  • Scale Removal: Powerful dispersant prevents Calcium and Iron deposition and aids in existing scale removal.
  • Energy Efficiency: Keeps heat transfer at peak by preventing corrosion and maintaining clean surfaces.

Technical Profile

  • Compatibility: Soft Water Systems
  • Primary Use: Internal Treatment
  • Action: Anti-scalant & Dispersant

Cooling Tower Management

AQUADET-131

Oxidizing Biocide

Stabilized Bromine-based biocide for broad-spectrum microbiological control.

Performance Benefits

  • Effective in ammonia-contaminated environments where chlorine fails.
  • Stable and effective even in high pH (Alkaline) cooling water.
  • Environmentally friendly, white to pale yellow liquid.
Standard Dosage: 30 PPM based on makeup water, twice weekly or as required by system load.

Technical Profile

  • Chemical Basis: Stabilized Bromine
  • Appearance: White to Pale Yellow
  • Miscibility: Complete in water

RO Membrane & Membrane Care

AQUADET-141

Premium RO Antiscalant

Polymer-free organic antiscalant for Reverse Osmosis and Nano Filtration.

Performance Benefits

  • Controls Carbonate and Sulphate scales efficiently.
  • Stabilizes Silica up to 120 PPM in reject water.
  • Reduces cleaning frequency and prolongs membrane life.
Recommended Dose: 2 to 5 PPM in feed water.

Technical Profile

  • Silica Tolerance: Up to 120 PPM
  • Application: RO & NF Membranes
  • Dose Point: Prior to Micron Filter

AQUADET-144

Acidic Membrane Cleaner

Specialized for removing insoluble sulphates (Barium, Strontium, Calcium) and Carbonates.

CIP Protocol Details

  • Concentration: 3-4% solution based on total system volume.
  • Procedure: Circulate for 1 hour; soak for 24+ hours for severe Calcium Sulfate scaling.
  • Flow Rates: 4" Membrane: 3-4 m³/hr | 8" Membrane: 8-10 m³/hr.

Technical Profile

  • pH (1% Soln): < 2.0
  • CIP Pressure: 3-4 Kg/cm²
  • Shelf Life: 12 Months (Cold/Shaded)
